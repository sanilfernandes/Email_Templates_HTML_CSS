GET 4th-READY RECIPES FOR ALL
THE MARG LOVERS OUT THERE

The 4th is quickly approaching! Now’s the time to prep and plan a few fresh cocktail recipes before your guests arrive. Of course, we're thinking margs - three different ones to be exact, from classic to completely fruit filled. Check out the recipes below!

Feeling Spicy MARGARITA

2 PARTS HORNITOS plata
1/2 PART AGAVE SYRUP
3/4 PART PINEAPPLE JUICE
1 PART LIME JUICE
2 jalapeno slices

Muddle jalapeno in shaker. Add rest
of ingredients and ice, then shake.
Strain into glass over fresh ice.

MANGO MARGARITA

2 PARTS HORNITOS plata
3/4 PART MANGO SYRUP
3/4 PART LIME JUICE

Combine ingredients in a shaker
with ice, shake, and strain into a
rocks glass over fresh ice. Garnish
with lime wheel and Tajin rim.

PINEAPPLE MARGARITA

1 1/2 PARTS HORNITOS REPOSADO
1/2 PART AGAVE SYRUP
1 PART PINEAPPLE JUICE
3/4 PART LIME JUICE

Combine ingredients in a shaker
with ice, shake, and strain into a
rocks glass over fresh ice. Garnish
with pineapple wedge.
